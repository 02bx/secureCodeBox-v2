Dispatcher environment deployed.

By default no ScanJobDefinitions or ParseJobDefinitions are included by default.
You can deploy the all ScanJobDefinitions included directly by the secureCodeBox with the `securecodebox/default-scan-jobs` chart.
If you don't want all ScanJobs you can deploy the scanners in isolation. e.g. `securecodebox/nmap`

To list all available ScanJobDefinitions run:
$ kubectl get get scanjobdefinitions.experimental.securecodebox.io
or in short:
$ kubectl get sjd

To list all available ParseJobDefinitions run:
$ kubectl get parsejobdefinitions.experimental.securecodebox.io
or in short:
$ kubectl get pjd
